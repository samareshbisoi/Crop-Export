Ragi


Crop Name Finger Millet (Ragi)
Suitable Varieties Bhairabi, Chilika, Arjun, Kalua, Subra, AKP-3, AKP-7, Kalyani, Godawari
Land Situation Well drained Upland in Kharif, Medium land in Rabi
Field Preparation Deep ploughing cum shallow harrowing for Fine tilth
Apply FYM / compost before forming beds
Sowing/Planting Time June-July
Seed Rate 8 kg/ha (direct sowing)
4kg/ha (Line Transplanting)
Spacing Row to Row: 20 - 25 cm
Plant to Plant: 8 - 10 cm
Fertilizer Recommendation 50:40:25 kg N:P2O5:K2O/ha with 4 ton of FYM/ha
Weed Management Herbicides like Butachlor 1.25 kg as pre-emergence for transplanted crop or Pre-emergence (within 2 DAS) Isoproturon 0.75kga.i./hand manual weeding at 30 days after emergence
For direct seeded post-emergence 2,4 D Na salt @0.5 kg 10 days after crop germination
Water Management Crop water requirement: 400-600 mm
In absence of rains irrigate the crop at tillering, flowering stages
Plant Protection Blast: (Mancozeb @2.5g/l water or Tricyclazole @0.6g /l of water
Suitable Farm Machinery Cultivator, Ragi seed drill, Sprayer, Ragi Thresher
Yield 20-25 q/ha
