Rice

Crop Name Rice
Suitable varieties Short Duration: Khandagiri, Hira, Jyotrimaye, Sahabhagidhan Medium Duration: Nabina, Lalat, Pratiksya, Swarna, Hiranmayee Long Duration: CR 1014, CR 1080, Panidhan, Upahar, Ashutosh, Hasant
Land situation Short Duration: Mostly Upland Medium Duration: Mostly Medium land Long Duration: Mostly low land
Field preparation After summer ploughing, 2-3 ploughing are required to prepare the soil. Do proper bunding of field to conserve and retain water.
Sowing time June, December-January
Seed rate 25-30 kg/ha
Spacing Row to Row: 20 to 25 cm Plant to Plant: 10-15 cm
Fertilizer Recommendation Kharif Rice: 60:30:30 kg N:P2O5:K2O/ha Rabi Rice: 80:40:40 kgÂ  N:P2O5:K2O/ha with 5 ton of FYM/ha
Weed management Mix Pretilachlore 30.7% EC 1.25lt/ha with 15-20kg sand and broadcasting within 48 hours of sowing
Water Management Crop water requirement: Short Duration: 700-750 mm Medium Duration: 900-1000 mm Long Duration: 1000-1200 mm In absence of rains irrigate the crop at tillering , panicle initiation, flowering and grain filling stage
Plant protection Disease Blast: Carbendazim 50WP @ 500g/ha (or) Tricyclozole 75 WP @ 500g/ha Bacterial Leaf Blight: Thiamethoxam 25 WDG 100g/ha Imidacloprid 17.8 SL 100ml/ha Sheath Blight: Carbendazim 50 WP @ 500g/ha Azoxystrobin @ 500ml/ha
Pest: Stemborer: Azadirachtin 0.03% @1000 ml/ha Carbofuran 3% CG @25 kg/ha Gall midge: Carbosulfan 25% EC @800-1000 ml/ha Thiamethoxam 25% WG @100 g/ha Leaf folder: Thiamethoxam 25% WG @100 kg/ha Triazophos 40% EC @625-1250 ml/ha BPH: Imidacloprid 17.8 SL @100-125 ml/ha Pymetrozine 50 WG@300g/ha
Suitable farm machinery Disc Plough, Cultivator, Rotavator, Transplanter, Sprayer, Reaper, Thresher
Expected yield 40-45 q/ha
